Common questions

What does the Fishing Pack include?
Real DNR lake depth contours, NOAA charts, depth-at-tap, launch + parking intel, and local rules & records — plus it removes all ads. One-time purchase, yours forever.

I bought the Fishing Pack on another device / reinstalled — how do I get it back?
It's tied to your Apple ID. Reinstall the app and it restores automatically, or tap "Restore Purchases" on the Fishing Pack screen — or email us and we'll help.

Where are my saved trips?
Right on your device — no account needed. Use Share or GPX export to get a trip out into Google Earth, OnX, Gaia, or AllTrails.

Why does the app ask for my location?
Only to show you on the map and track your adventures while you're using it. Your location never leaves your phone. See our privacy policy.
