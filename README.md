## What it does

- 🗺️ **Three map styles** — Streets, Topo (OnX-style contour lines), and Satellite
- 🔍 **Search anything** — beaches, boat launches, rivers, trailheads, parks
- 📍 **One-tap discovery** — Trails, Parking, Overlooks, Beaches, Boat Launches, Waterfalls, Camping, Picnic & Fishing spots near you
- 🚗 **Drive there** — every pin hands off to Google Maps navigation
- ▶ **Track your adventure** — Hike, Wave Runner, 4-Wheeler, Boat, Kayak, Bike, Snowmobile & more, with live time / miles / speed / max speed
- 💾 **Save & share** — share a summary + GPX file (opens in Google Earth, OnX, Gaia, AllTrails)
- 🔑 **Map Key** — explains every color and symbol on whichever map style you're on
- ❓ **What's this?** — tap any spot on the map and SkyDog identifies it
- 📴 **No accounts, no tracking, no subscription.** One file. Your GPS data stays on your phone.

## Put it on your phone

1. Open **https://skydog84.github.io/skydog-gps/** in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android)
2. Tap **Share → Add to Home Screen**
3. It installs with the SkyDog AI logo as its icon and runs full-screen like a real app

## Tech

Single-file HTML/JS with a custom-built zero-dependency map engine (canvas tile renderer,
pinch-zoom, custom markers & popups). Map data © [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/copyright)
contributors; topo tiles © OpenTopoMap; satellite imagery © Esri. Verified by a 39-test
automated browser suite (simulated GPS, tracking, GPX export, offline resilience).
